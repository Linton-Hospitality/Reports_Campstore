Firewood — The Revenue You Can't Give Away
07 — What Firewood Pricing Actually Means

2,249 bundles sold over 9 months. Firewood is 51.6% of all store revenue — $22,490 net. At $4.00/bundle cost and $10 retail, the margin is 60%. That makes firewood not just the highest-revenue item in the store, but also the highest-margin. Every bundle given away for free costs $6.00 in gross profit. Franchisees who include firewood in the nightly rate or comp it routinely are forfeiting their single biggest profit driver.
